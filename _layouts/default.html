About Us

Seattle Hacks is a community-driven resource for the developer community in Seattle, maintained by developers for developers. Feel free to post information about hackathons, hack spaces, workshops, conferences, meetups or anything else that you think would be of interest to developers. Contributing is easy, just submit a pull request! Hit us up @SeattleHacks if you have any questions or feedback.
